Books & Learning Resources

Created by teachers, made for curious learners

Willow & Bloom Publishing is the imprint of authors and educators Matthew Graham and Rebecca Whitlock — creating books and resources that make learning a joy.

About us

We're a husband-and-wife team of teachers with a shared passion for learning and development. Through Willow & Bloom Publishing we create books and educational resources designed to support children, families, and fellow educators — bringing classroom experience to every page.

The authors

Rebecca Whitlock

Author & Educator

A teacher passionate about learning and development, Rebecca writes warm, reassuring guides that support children through every milestone of growing up — from first periods and puberty to friendships and big emotions.

Matthew Graham

Author & Educator

A teacher who believes learning should spark curiosity. Matthew creates fun, fact-filled books and early-reader collections that help children build confidence, fluency, and a genuine love of reading.

Early readers & learning

Reading collections and comprehension workbooks that build fluency, confidence, and a love of learning — aligned to first and second grade standards.

Growing up & confidence guides

Warm, practical guides that help children navigate puberty, emotions, and friendships with confidence.
